Fuspay

Fuspay is a simple payment solution that allows you make payments without the risk of using your ATM card details. Our unique technology makes it possible for you to complete your payment without worrying about your ATM card details been stolen or your account been hacked.

Fuspay gives you the luxury and flexibility of completing your transactions with just a token generated on your mobile. Now can pay for anything, anywhere at any time of the day. Gone are the days of long waits at POS, praying for transactions not to fail and that drama.

We have crated a very fast, smooth and flexible system that makes it possible for you as a business owner to receive payment in less than a hour. Your account gets credited in just a matter of seconds. All your worries about transaction failure and credit complications are completely erased with Fuspay.

Using Fuspay means more money in your pocket, we believe you don’t have to give too much to get much, which is why using our service has been made very affordable. The best part is that you don’t have to pay a sign-up fee or wait for eternity to integrate our technology, you can get started in a couples of minutes. All you need to do is sign up, create an account, create your secured payment link, sit back and wait for your money to come knocking.
